The planning committee is thrilled to see the topics coming through in our proposal submission for the 2013 event! There is still plenty of time to send yours in. We wanted to point out to prospective presenters that, though our focus is on topics targeting to the growth and development of peer mentors, we also have increasing attendance from program staff and faculty. We have had some valuable sessions in the past for attendees who oversee mentoring components on assessment, training, and best practices. Be sure to note the audience of your session, whether it is for peer mentors, program staff/faculty, or both.

Also, the Mentoring Symposium is a great venue for our undergraduate and graduate mentors to gain experience presenting on a national level. We encourage students to submit either alone or with their component staff/faculty. Some of our best sessions have been facilitated by student mentors!

We look forward to more submissions. In the meantime, feel free to contact us with any questions.
