Save the Date

The 2014 National Mentoring Symposium will be held on [DATE], 2014 in Indianapolis at the IUPUI Campus Center. Be sure to mark the date in your calendar for this one-day national conference for students who work as peer mentors in various capacities. This event allows for peer mentoring programs from around the nation to come together in an effort of personal and professional development. Peer mentors, program staff, and faculty will enhance their skills and increase their understanding of strategic methods to mentor students.


Now Accepting Proposals

The National Mentoring Symposium planning committee is now accepting proposals for poster sessions and concurrent sessions. Presentation topics can include professionalism, diversity, leadership, motivation, time management, and marketing mentoring skills. While these subjects are priority, we encourage you to submit any original ideas you may have for presentation topics at this year's symposium.
