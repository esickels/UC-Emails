Hello from the Diversity Enrichment and Achievement Program (DEAP)!

Hello from the Diversity Enrichment and Achievement Program (DEAP)! If you are not aware, DEAP is a program within University College dedicated to supporting the academic success of diverse students here at IUPUI.

You are receiving this message because you are a current IUPUI student who has not yet enrolled for the spring 2015 semester.

We are here to help you work through what has delayed your enrollment for the spring semester and assist you in any way we can with completing your education.

How can we be of assistance? Please contact the DEAP office at deap@iupui.edu or (317) 274-2958 to speak to us or schedule an appointment.

It is my hope that your registration for spring is just an oversight and that you will indeed be joining us again this spring semester. However, if you don’t have plans of returning, we would like to know that as well!
