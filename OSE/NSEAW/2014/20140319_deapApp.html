Congratulations on your admission to IUPUI!

The Diversity Enrichment & Achievement Program (DEAP) commends you on your success and welcomes you to the class of 2018. At IUPUI, we celebrate diversity and recognize that students of color often follow a unique journey through their educational experience. Your commitment and hard work is evident, and we would like to invite you to join our family of scholars in the Diversity Enrichment & Achievement Program.

College is a whole new world! DEAP is here to help you navigate the university system and achieve your goals. Participating in DEAP will provide you with academic resources, support, and adventures while engaging you in cultural programming, leadership development, and service learning opportunities. To be a funded DEAP scholar, you must:

  • Be one of the first 50 to return the included application.
  • Participate in the Summer Bridge Program.
  • Continue to be an active DEAP scholar during your freshman year at IUPUI.

Because funded participation is limited, it is important to reserve an orientation and apply for housing as soon as possible (if planning to live on campus). Follow the steps below in order to participate in DEAP.

  1. Create your IUPUI technology accounts
  2. Pay your enrollment deposit of $100
  3. Reserve an orientation
  4. Apply for housing right away to get into your preferred dormitory
  5. Take your placement tests prior to attending orientation
  6. AT ORIENTATION, sign up to participate in the Summer Bridge Program
  7. Start checking your IUPUI e-mail account for future communications including welcome events, scholarship opportunities, etc.

Know that we are here to serve you and help make your transition to IUPUI as smooth as possible. If you have any questions at all, please feel free to call (317) 278-6058 or e-mail angeande@iupui.edu. DEAP is here to help make your graduation goal a reality! Let’s do this!
