To our loyal supporters

We hope you're having a great summer so far. Since we're in the middle of wedding season, we'd like to share a unique giving opportunity. We've had some brides ask us if they could donate to Sacred Heart Breast Cancer Research in lieu of wedding favors.

The answer is YES!

We'll even provide you with table cards, pink ribbons and thank-you cards for your guests. A donation to breast cancer research is wonderful way to give back at your wedding, especially if you or a loved on has been affected by breast cancer.

With gratitude,

The SHBCR team
