We hope you are enjoying your summer. As you and your student continue to plan ahead for the fall semester, we wanted to remind you of some important resources and the student programs that will be taking place at the beginning of the semester to help you and your student with the transition to college.

Check out Weeks of Welcome (WOW)!

Encourage your student to attend one of the 28 events during Weeks of Welcome (WOW) August 23—September 7, 2014. WOW is one of the longest standing campus traditions. Students have the opportunity to connect with resources, meet new friends, and create a network of support at IUPUI. WOW has a variety of programs including the Campus Recreation festival, the Student Involvement Expo, diversity programming, community service, and the 37th Annual Ice Cream Social with distinguished dippers including the Chancellor, deans, and vice chancellors.

Check out the Student Affairs website!

IUPUI student orientation introduces students and families to a variety of campus resources, student organizations, and leadership opportunities to showcase what it means to experience IUPUI. Visit the Division of Student Affairs website to learn more about the ways your student can get involved and meet new friends at IUPUI. More importantly, check out our parent and families website. It is a great way to support your student through their college experience!

Connect on Facebook!

IUPUI recognizes the value in staying connected to help you and your student succeed. We want to highlight opportunities for you both.

  • Encourage your student to join the Class of 2018 Facebook page to become part of the Jaguar community and meet fellow incoming Jaguars.
  • The IUPUI Jaguar Family Association Facebook page is a way for you, as families, to stay connected with what is happening on campus, interact, ask questions, and get advice from others.

If you have any questions, please contact us at jagfam@iupui.edu or at (317) 274-4431.
