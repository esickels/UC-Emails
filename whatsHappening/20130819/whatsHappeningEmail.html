1. Part-Time Job Fair

Spend all your money on textbooks? Come out to the Student Employment Part-Time Job Fair to meet with employers who have hourly, work-study, and on- and off-campus opportunities for students. You'll also learn about JagJobs, the Office of Student Employment, and other resources to help you earn some extra money this semester. LEARN MORE
When: Thursday, August 22, 10 a.m.–1 p.m.
Where: University College/Taylor Hall Courtyard

When: Tuesdays at 5:00 p.m.
Where: Taylor Hall, UC 115
